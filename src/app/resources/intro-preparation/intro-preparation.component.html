I. Improve Listening and Reading Skills

1
Listen to the recordings in the “Main Content” and try to guess the main idea of the reading.
2
Click on unfamiliar words in the reading to know how the words are used in context with the help of the dictionary.

II. Effectively Improve and Expand Your Vocabulary

1
Use the filter function in “Main Content” to select level 3 words. Skim through the reading to find unfamiliar words. This allows you to quickly find level-appropriate words that you can learn and expand your vocabulary.
2
Click on a word or phrase to activate Ponds and expand your knowledge. You can quickly check for any possible key points you’ve missed.
(When opening Ponds, please make sure to switch off words that are above your current level.)
a. Word Pond- allows you to find words or phrases that often appear together in different contexts. You can quickly identify unfamiliar word combinations and learn how to use them.
b. Character Pond- you can find out how a character is used extensively to form other words or phrases. You can quickly identify unfamiliar words or phrases, form new phrases with ease, and learn how to use them.
c. Radical Pond- you can see other frequently used characters with the same radical, find unfamiliar characters, improve your memory, and enhance your ability to recognize characters.

3
Click on “Exercises.” Then, use “Word Puzzle,” “Match the Pinyin,” and “Match the Meaning” to test your vocabulary learning result.

III. Improve Grammar Mastery

1
Click on “Grammar” and use the filter function to display grammar points of level 3 and below.
2
Read the grammar explanations and the sample sentences (with recordings) carefully so that you can fully understand the usages.
3
Click on “Exercises” and check your learning results with “Drag to Fill.”
